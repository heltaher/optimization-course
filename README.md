## 📚 Course Content

The course is structured into modules, progressing from foundational concepts to more advanced techniques:

*   **Introduction to Optimization:** What is an optimization problem? Classifications (linear, nonlinear, convex, non-convex), and examples.
*   **Fundamentals of Linear Programming (LP):** The simplex method, duality theory, and sensitivity analysis.
*   **Unconstrained Nonlinear Optimization:** Optimality conditions, gradient descent, Newton's method, and conjugate gradients.
*   **Constrained Nonlinear Optimization:** Lagrange multipliers, Karush-Kuhn-Tucker (KKT) conditions, and interior-point methods.
*   **Heuristic and Metaheuristic Algorithms:** Genetic Algorithms (GA), Simulated Annealing, and Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) for complex, non-convex problems.
*   **Applications:** Real-world examples from fields like machine learning, operations research, and engineering.
